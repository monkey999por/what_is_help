Cafe Debut(h1)

<p>baser CMS Cafe site theme
Baser CMS Theme Contest 2012 Restaurant Theme Award (Click here for award results ）

A cafe site theme that you can start right now!(h2)

<p>baserCMS is a cake PHP-based domestic CMS with the slogan "Perfect for corporate sites". Cafe Debut was created by Team Komorikomasha to enter the BaseCMS Theme Contest 2012.

<p>After installing baserCMS and initializing the Cafe Debut theme, all you have to do is take a photo on Instagram on your smartphone.

<p>This is a photolog-style baserCMS theme that allows you to quickly create a wonderful cafe site with stylish Instagram photos.

Link with Instagram via plug-in(h3)

<p>By linking with Instagram with a plug-in, photos taken on Instagram will be immediately reflected on the top page.

Responsive design for comfortable viewing on smartphones and tablets(h3)

<p>Responsive design has been adopted to support display on various devices.It's nice to be able to take a picture of the restaurant's menu on Instagram and check it on your smartphone right away♪

Because it is a web font, decorative characters and icons are also beautiful.(h3)

<p>Site title, navigation, and main headings are Google Web Font and icons are web fonts (Fontello). We were able to achieve beautiful text and icon display without worrying about resolution even on scaled smartphones and tablets.
